

# Guidelines
**Professional MBA Program Overview:**

- Students will complete a total of **42 credits** required for the Professional MBA Degree:
    - 30 Core Credits (MBAC)
    - 12 Elective Credits (MBAX/MSBX)
        - **Year one: 18 credits:**
            - Summer 2025, Fall 2025, and Spring 2026: 6 credits each term
        - **Year two: 24 credits:**
            - Summer 2026: 9 credits
            - Fall 2026: average 6 elective credits
            - Spring 2027: average 6 elective credits + 3 core credits for Capstone project engaged over the course of the second year.

**Additional Academic Policies:**

- Professional MBA students cannot take courses from another (non-Leeds) graduate program for credit towards the MBA degree.
- Students must maintain a cumulative GPA of a 3.00 or higher in order to remain in **good academic standing**.
- Students must have a cumulative GPA of a 3.00 or higher to be awarded the MBA degree.
- Students must earn a C or higher in order for a grade to count for degree credit.
- A grade of C- or below in any course will _**not**_ count for degree credit and must be repeated.
- For a full list of academic policies and procedures, please refer to the [**Leeds Graduate Student Handbook**.](https://canvas.colorado.edu/courses/118359/files/77813409?wrap=1 "Grad Programs Student Handbook all programs_FINAL to upload.pdf")
